About BitChess

What is BitChess.com?

BitChess(.com) is a website dedicated to chess and the millions of chess players around the globe who love the game. It is a place to share what you think, improve your chess game and enjoy every aspect of chess!

Who owns BitChess(.com) Who runs the site?

BitChess.com is "powered by" some lovely students! This is a special project for the ones who loves the game. You are welcome to contribute with articles, blogs, in the forums, and more.

What are the site's policies?

Be nice, no cheating! BitChess.com is a friendly community. Please be on your best behavior as you play chess and interact with others. Failure to follow these rules will result in your account being closed. There should be no abusive language, personal attacks, threats, or any other unkind behavior. You may not post any offensive content on Chess.com including your username, avatar, or personal description. You may not add any offensive comments, chat, or other content. The following behavior is prohibited and can result in your being kicked or banned: cheating, using offensive/vulgar language, using toilet humor, making personal attacks, spamming or advertising competitive websites, religious or political debate, intentionally pointless/distracting posts, and discussion of illegal activities (drugs, etc).

What is the meaning of the rank

The rank represents your position in the general classment. By winning more games you rank increases. Also, your rank decreseas by losing some chess games. There are also rank for clubs.

Info about users and accounts

Can I share computers with another member?

Yes! Family, siblings, friends etc are all allowed to play here from the same computer (and ip address) as long as each person playing here 'Must' have and use their own account.

How do I close / delete my account?

We're very sorry that you wish to leave. However, if you would like to close your account entirely, please click here.

How can I change my name?

If you would like to change your name but keep your current account hover your cursor over your avatar in the left navigation menu and select Settings from the drop down menu that appears. Also you can not change your username. Note that usernames are a minimum of 3 characters and a maximum of 20 and you cannot have any spaces or dots in the name, nor any special characters like ^ & * ! " L $ % ( ) + = ? / \ | It can be either one whole word, or use a dash (-) or underscore (_) as a separator.
